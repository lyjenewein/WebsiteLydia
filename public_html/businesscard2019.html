Graphic Profile. Modern.


At school we were given the opportunity of designing our own businesscards, that the school will print.
My idea was to create a vertical businesscard instead of a classic landscape one.

CONCEPT

DESIGN

For me it was very important to keep up my graphic profile. On my website I have four colors threading through. I wanted to include these colors, without making it look too colorful. So I decided to go with a "no signal"...PC! The PC is photoshopped in the same way as my mockups on the Web Design page. I created a 2-sided and 1-sided version.
I created the design with InDesign.
