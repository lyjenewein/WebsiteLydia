G. Raff Skincare


An ad campaign that I wanted to stand out. When you watch the short ads you don't expect that it is actually an ad for... skincare! The brand is made up and i created the logo and crafted some skincare products with the new look. I wanted this campaign to have a specific song with a good beat - and edit the video fitting to the music. It is meant to convey the message that if you use products by G. Raff your life feels like a music video.
We had a lot of fun filming, with GoPro, drone and a systemcamera we got a lot of different shots. The short 10 second videos are in two aspect ratios: 4:3 and 9:16 - for instagram post and instagram story. Then there is a 36 second main video that explains it a bit more.
