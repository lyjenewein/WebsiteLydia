Logo Design.
Complementary.


This design was created during a course I made (Digitalt Skapande / Digital Creation). Bubbens bar is a completely made-up vintage bar, and our dog stood model for the logo. I wanted to make the colors really stand out so I decided to go with complementary colors - blue and orange. There are two different versions - a more detailed one and a simpler one.

I made the logo in Inkscape as a SVG or vector graphic - so it is scalable without becoming pixelated. It can be used for small prints like business card or bigger prints like posters.
