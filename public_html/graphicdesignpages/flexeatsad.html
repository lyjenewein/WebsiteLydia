Motion Graphics.


This project was a lot of fun! Aaand took a lot of time. But I am so happy in the result that it was abolutely worth it. I started with thinking of the idea. I knew I wanted to make an ad for Flexeats, so I wanted to create food that changes into different food. It was thought to be a social media ad, so I needed something quick and bright to get the viewers attentions.

After pen & paper sketches and deciding what food turns into which, I created the different food in Adobe Illustrator with simple shapes. I had to make sure that all things I wanted to move are on different layers and to make it easy for later - that the layers are named correctly.
After creating the illustrations I imported them into a new After Effects project and then went food by food - layer by layer - keyframe by keyframe!

The ad exists in different aspect ratios: 1:1 for the classical Instagram ad/feed and 9:16 for Instagram TV.
