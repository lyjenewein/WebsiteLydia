A humans best friend


Do you have a dog? Are you bored of going the same route? Then I made a website exactly for you! I am the proud owner of a dog that has a lot of energy and is always up for new adventures. I gathered destinations and activites in Skåne and what to think about if you want to go hiking with your dog on my website TRAILBUDDIES.



I decided to make this website since it is sometimes hard to find the right places or get good tips on where to go with your dog. I visited all of the places written about, and give little tips and tricks.

This website was made with Wordpress.
I think Wordpress can be a very good tool to make good looking, effective websites.
